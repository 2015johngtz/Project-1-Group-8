About Us!

Welcome to Neighborhood WatchDog! We provide a powerful tool that generates valuable insights for different neighborhoods and properties across the United States. By inputting a zip code, users gain access to local facts, crime rates, and available property details from the Zillow API.

Our app aims to empower users with knowledge about areas of interest. It provides comprehensive information on property photos, property prices, property details, and crime rates to help users make informed decisions about relocation, property investments, or general curiosity about neighborhoods.

Join us on this journey and let our web app become your trusted companion in the search for neighborhood information and real estate opportunities.

We value your feedback as we continue to enhance and refine our app. If you have any additional questions feel free to contact us from our portfolios down below. Thank you for choosing us, and we look forward to assisting you in your quest for knowledge and property exploration!
